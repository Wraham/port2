About me

Hi! I'm Wasil Raham, an 18-year-old passionate web designer and video/photo editor driven by creativity and the art of storytelling. With over a year of experience, I specialize in building responsive and visually stunning websites and crafting high-quality photo and video edits that captivate audiences.
